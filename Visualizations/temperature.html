Max Temperatures

There is only one significant relationship found between lattitude and the four weather measurments that we analyzed. Temperature follows as one would expect, getting warmer as the lattidue approaches zero. As one would expect, the weather becomes warmer the closer we come to the equator. There do exist outliers in the southern equator which can likely be attributed to arid climate zones. This could be further studied with our data set. We can also garner from the temperature graph that in January the northern lattitudes are very cold and the southern latidues remain above freezing. This is consistant with winter being in the the northern hemisphere.
